## üîç SEO IMPACT ANALYSIS

### What Google Sees as Duplicate:
- ‚ùå Identical paragraphs across pages
- ‚ùå Same H2/H3 headings with no unique content
- ‚ùå Copy-paste sections with only state name swapped

### What Google Sees as Unique:
- ‚úÖ Different primary keywords (state names)
- ‚úÖ Unique industry descriptions
- ‚úÖ Different city names throughout
- ‚úÖ State-specific testimonials
- ‚úÖ Varying OSHA penalty amounts
- ‚úÖ State-specific FAQs

### Our Strategy:
**30% Unique + 70% Standardized = No Duplicate Penalty**

The state-specific content (industries, cities, testimonials, state FAQs) provides enough uniqueness that Google will see these as distinct pages targeting different geographic keywords.
