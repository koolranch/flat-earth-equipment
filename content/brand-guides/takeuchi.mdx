# Takeuchi Construction Equipment Guide

Takeuchi manufactures compact excavators, skid steer loaders, compact track loaders, and wheel loaders known for durability and innovative design in the compact construction equipment market.

## Serial Number & Data Plate Location

**Common Locations:**
- **Compact Excavators (TB Series)**: Right side of upper frame, accessible from ground level
- **Skid Steers (TS Series)**: Right side frame, near operator compartment
- **Compact Track Loaders (TL Series)**: Right side of machine frame
- **Wheel Loaders (TW Series)**: Engine compartment or side frame rail

**Plate Information:**
- Model designation and series (TB, TS, TL, TW)
- Serial number and identification number
- Engine specifications and tier rating
- Manufacturing date and plant information
- Operating weight and rated operating capacity

**⚠️ Verification Required:** Always verify serial number information with official Takeuchi documentation or your local dealer before making critical decisions.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I decode a Takeuchi serial number?**

**Q: What do the model prefixes mean?**
A: TB = compact excavators, TS = skid steer loaders, TL = compact track loaders, TW = wheel loaders.

**Q: Where can I find fault code information?**

**Q: How do I access the diagnostic menu?**

**Q: What maintenance should I perform regularly?**
A: Follow the maintenance schedule in your operator manual, including engine oil changes, hydraulic filter replacement, and greasing points.
