# New Holland Construction Equipment Guide

New Holland Construction produces reliable skid steer loaders, compact track loaders, compact wheel loaders, and compact excavators designed for versatility and performance.

## Serial Number & Data Plate Location

**Common Locations:**
- **Skid Steers (L-Series)**: Right side frame, near operator compartment
- **Compact Track Loaders (C-Series)**: Right side of machine frame
- **Compact Excavators (E-Series)**: Right side of upper frame
- **Wheel Loaders**: Engine compartment or frame rail

**Plate Information:**
- Model designation (L, C, or E prefix)
- Serial number and PIN
- Manufacturing date and plant code
- Engine specifications and emissions tier
- Operating weight and rated capacity

**⚠️ Verification Required:** Always verify serial number information with official New Holland documentation or your local dealer before making critical decisions.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I decode a New Holland serial number?**
A: New Holland serial numbers contain model and production information. Use our [New Holland serial lookup tool](/parts/construction-equipment-parts/new-holland-skid-steer-serial-number-lookup) for detailed decoding.

**Q: What's the difference between L-Series and C-Series?**
A: L-Series are skid steer loaders with wheels, while C-Series are compact track loaders with rubber tracks for improved traction and ground protection.

**Q: Where can I find fault code information?**
A: Visit our [New Holland fault codes section](/brand/new-holland/fault-codes) for comprehensive diagnostic information.

**Q: How do I perform a diagnostic self-test?**
A: Access the service menu and follow the self-test procedure. Exact steps vary by model and year - refer to your operator manual.

**Q: What maintenance intervals should I follow?**
A: Follow the maintenance schedule in your operator manual. Key intervals include daily pre-operation checks, regular fluid changes, and filter replacements.

## Model Series Information

- Vertical lift or radial lift configurations
- Various bucket and attachment options
- Tier 4 emissions compliance

**C-Series Compact Track Loaders:**
- Rubber track undercarriage
- Enhanced flotation and traction
- Reduced ground pressure
