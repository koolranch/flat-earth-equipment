Q: Where is the Toyota forklift serial plate?
A: Typically on the dash cowl or frame near the counterweight; some include a stamped frame ID.

Q: Can I get the exact year from a Toyota serial?
A: Use our serial tool ranges; many series require matching serial breaks to estimate build year.

Q: What's the E A5-1 code?
A: Accelerator sensor mismatch; inspect the pedal sensor/harness and confirm dual-channel agreement.
