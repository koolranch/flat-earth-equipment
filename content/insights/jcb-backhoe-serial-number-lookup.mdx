<li><strong>Curb Side of Frame Rail:</strong> Under the engine compartment opening</li>

<h3>Alternative Identification Marks</h3>
<p>Apart from the VIN, JCB backhoes may have other identification marks including the PIN (Product Identification Number) and engine number. </p>
<p>These alternatives can often be found on a type plate or tag attached to different parts of the machine. </p>
<p>Common locations include the hydraulic control housing and near the engine itself.</p>
<p><strong>Key Alternative Identifiers:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>PIN:</strong> Often on a type plate</li>
<li><strong>Engine Number:</strong> Stamped near the engine</li>
