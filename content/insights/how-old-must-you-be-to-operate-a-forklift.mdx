Operating a forklift requires both skill and responsibility. In most U.S. workplaces, you must be at least 18 years old and OSHA-certified.

![Forklift driver age requirement](https://koala.sh/api/image/v2-jsj8g-zqg9r.jpg?width=1216&height=832&dream)

## OSHA Age & Training Requirements

- **Minimum age**: 18 (non-agriculture), 16 (agriculture with exceptions)
- **Training**: Required by OSHA, must include hands-on
- **Certification**: Must be documented by employer
