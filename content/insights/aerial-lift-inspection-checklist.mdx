<h3>Key Takeaways for Your Inspection Program</h3>
<p>The most crucial insight is that no single solution fits every operation. Your choice depends on your fleet&#39;s size, the technical savvy of your team, and your documentation requirements.</p>

<li><p><strong>For large, multi-site operations:</strong> Digital platforms like SafetyCulture (iAuditor) or GoCanvas offer unparalleled advantages. </li>

<li><p><strong>For high-turnover or mixed-equipment environments:</strong> Physical tag systems from Brady (Scafftag) or XpressTags are incredibly effective.
