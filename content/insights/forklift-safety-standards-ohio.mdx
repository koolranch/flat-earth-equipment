Forklift operators in Ohio must comply with OSHA regulations and state labor laws governing industrial equipment safety.

## Key Safety Requirements

- Forklift operators must be certified
- Daily inspections are required
- Employers must keep safety records

## State-Level Enforcement

In Ohio, OSHA enforcement is federal. Local municipalities may apply additional requirements for warehouses and public-facing job sites.
