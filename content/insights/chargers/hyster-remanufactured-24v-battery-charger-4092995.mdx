### What “Remanufactured” Means for This Charger

Remanufacturing goes beyond a simple repair. Each unit is:

1. **Disassembled and inspected** – Worn or marginal parts are replaced
2. **Updated to current specs** – Where applicable, components are upgraded to current production standards
3. **Tested** – Full electrical and thermal testing before shipping
4. **Documented** – You receive a unit backed by our warranty and support

The result is a charger that performs like new, with a lower price and a smaller environmental footprint. That’s the same mindset behind responsible electronics recycling: when equipment can be reused, reuse it first.
