<p>Purchasing an EP forklift involves not only the initial price but also ongoing costs. The initial investment for a forklift can range significantly based on model and specifications. <h3>Maintenance and Servicing</h3>
<p>Regular maintenance of forklifts is vital to ensure reliability and safety. EP Equipment highlights the importance of consistent care, which extends the lifespan of the machinery. Regular maintenance can prevent costly repairs and improve the performance of the forklift.</p>
