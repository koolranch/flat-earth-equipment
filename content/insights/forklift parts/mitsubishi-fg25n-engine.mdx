The Mitsubishi FG25N is equipped with a GK21E engine. This engine can run on either gasoline or LPG. It provides a power output of approximately 58 hp (43 kW) at a speed of 2300 RPM.


The forklift's powertrain is designed to deliver strong performance for heavy-duty tasks. It utilizes a hydraulic transmission that ensures smooth operation and efficient load handling.
