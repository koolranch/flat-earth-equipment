 Description
Side Broom
 The side brooms purpose is to throw debris out in front of the sweeper so that the main broom  
can pick it up.
Main Broom
 The main broom is built around a heavy duty core, with staple-set tufts aligned in spiral rows.   
This design places bristles on the floor at all times to increase vacuum  efficiency and improve  
pickup characteristics.
Side Scrub “Rotary Brush”
 A rotary brush or “side scrubber” is designed to spin and agitate debris loose from the ground  
with its dens and stiff bristles
Cylindrical Scrub  “Main Scrub”
 Cylindrical Scrub or “Main Scrub” use a full-fill design that keeps bristles on the floor at all times 
and directs solution to the center of the scrubbing path as the bristles agitate the debris loose 
from the ground. The filament length on this brush designates it as a “scrubber” since the short 
bristle isn’t able to “flick” or “sweep” the debris off the ground.
Pad Driver
 Stiff, 1/2” long Polypropylene bristles unevenly trimmed and widely spaced to firmly grip  
scrubbing pads.

 Material Description
Nylon
 Nylon A highly productive filament and has an excellent brush life in dry sweeping conditions. Nylon performs well 
on rough surfaces and in high-temperature environments because of its durability and high melting point. The 
smaller the grit size the more aggressive the scrubbing.
Soft Nylon .18 - .20 Nylon bristle gauge
Medium 
Nylon
.22 Nylon bristle gauge
Stiff Nylon .28 Nylon bristle
Nylon Patrol The “V” shaped pattern of the bristles is used for sweeping large indoor and outdoor areas at higher speeds and 
gives superior hopper loading of sand and larger items.  
Nylon Wire A combination of Nylon and Wire to provide more stiffness.
Poly
 Poly The most popular filament in scrubbing applications, Poly is efficient for use with most soils and surfaces. 
Although Poly is more economical than Nylon, it is not recommended for sweeping high temperature debris.
Medium Poly .22 bristle gauge
Stiff Poly .28 bristle gauge
Poly Wire A combination of Poly and Wire to provide more stiffness.
Proex
 Proex Is highly efficient as an all-purpose filament for indoor and outdoor sweeping applications, including damp or 
humid environments. Not recommended for high-temperature sweeping applications. Proex is polypropylene 
and derives its name from the X-shape of the bristle.
Extra Stiff 
Proex
This Proex bristle gauge is greater than .28
Proex/Wire A mixture of Proex and Crimped Wire. This is the most versatile and widely used filament.
Stiff Proex/
Wire
A .28 gauge bristle mixture of Proex and Crimped Wire 
Wire
 Wire Crinkle wire used on side brooms and in main brooms with a combination of union and proex
Butcher Wire Scarifying brush that uses staple set butcher wire to cut through dirt and buildup on concrete floors and scratch 
the surface.  
Flat Wire Flat Wire is made from flattened spring steel wire and is an aggressive material used only on side brushes and 
primarily in outdoor applications.  This is an excellent material for digging heavy compacted debris away from the 
edges of buildings and curbs.  
Crimped Wire Used for applications needing extra cutting action for heavy-duty sweeping of compacted dirt or debris. Normally 
mixed with Proex or Union filaments. Recommended for low dust sweeping on unsealed surfaces when used 
alone.
Steel Wire (.014” dia.) is the most aggressive of the series, for use on unfinished concrete surfaces.
Union Fiber
 Union Fiber Comprised of a mixture of Tampico and Palmyra fibers, this material is highly efficient for dust control and 
sweeping fine debris. As a natural fiber, Union has relatively shorter wear life versus other synthetic materials.
Union Mix A combination of Palmyra and White Tampico used in hard surface polishing applications.
Union/Wire A combination of Union Fiber and Wire to provide more stiffness.
Grit
 Grit Nylon filament impregnated with silicon carbide particles. Grit filaments are offered in .070”/46, .050”/80, 
.035”/180, .022”/120, and .018”/500. The first number is the filament diameter. The larger the diameter the stiffer 
the bristle. The second number is the particle size. The smaller the grit number the more aggressive the scrubbing.
Also Available Bassine Bassine is used for general scrubbing purposes. Water absorption properties make this an excellent, low cost 
choice for hard surface scrubbing.
White 
Tampico
100% White Tampico is naturally acid resistant. Ideal for the marble polishing industry.

Snap Type Mounting type for the brushes which snap into place

When replacing the cylindrical brushes on your 
sweeper or scrubber, the suggestions below will 
give you longer life and maximum performance:
•  Remove the old brushes and install the new 
brushes following the directions on the 
equipment manual.
•  Start the machine and lower the brush until 
you can hear or feel it sweep the floor. Don’t 
lower the brush any further at this time. If you 
are installing the brushes on a scrubber, do not 
turn on the water.
•  Let the brush sweep the floor while you count 
to 20. Now raise the brush and move the 
machine away. You should see a clean strip 
on the floor where the brush made contact. 
This strip should be 1½" to 2" wide across its 
full length. If one end is wider than the other, 
the brush mounting needs adjustment. (Refer 
to your equipment manual.) Check the brush 
chamber to assure that the brush arms are free 
to move.
Cuando reemplaces los cepillos cilíndricos en 
tus cepilladores y barredoras, las siguientes 
sugerencias  te darán larga duración y 
funcionamiento máximo:
•   Quita los cepillos viejos e instala los nuevos
  cepillos siguiendo las direcciones de acuerdo  al 
manual de instalación.
•  Prende la máquina y baja el cepillos hasta   
 que puedas escuchar o sentir el cepillo  
  barrer el piso. En ese instante no bajes el   
 cepillo más abajo. Si estas instalando el   
 cepillo en un depurador, no abras el agua.
•  Deja el cepillo barrer el piso mientras cuentas 
20 segundos. Ahora levanta el cepillo y mueve 
la máquina a un lado. Deberás ver una marca 
limpia en el piso donde el cepillo hizo contacto. 
La marca deberá ser de 1½" a 2" de ancho a 
lo largo. Si un lado es más largo que el otro, 
el cepillo necesita ajustamiento (diríjase a su 
manual de instalación). Revisa el despacho 
del cepillo para asegurarse que los brazos del 
cepillos están libres para moverse.
Lors du remplacement des brosses cylindriques 
sur votre balai ou d'épuration, les suggestions 
ci-dessous vous donnera plus de vie et des 
performances maximales: 
•  Retirez les brosses usagées et installez les 
nouveaux balais en suivant les instructions sur le 
manuel de l'équipement. 
•  Démarrez la machine et abaisser la brosse jusqu'à 
ce que vous pouvez entendre ou sentir balayer 
le plancher. Ne baissez pas la brosse plus loin en 
ce moment. Si vous installez les pinceaux sur un 
laveur, ne mettez pas l'eau. 
•  Laisser la brosse à balayer le sol pendant que vous 
compter jusqu'à 20. Maintenant, augmenter le 
pinceau et éloigner la machine. Vous devriez voir 
une bande de nettoyage sur le sol où la brosse est 
entré en contact. Cette bande doit être de 1½ à 2 
po de largeur sur toute sa longueur. Si l'une des 
extrémités est plus large que l'autre, le pinceau de 
montage doit être ajusté. (Reportez-vous à votre 
manuel de l’équipement.) Vérifier la chambre de 
brosse pour assurer que le bras de balais sont 
libres de se déplacer.
